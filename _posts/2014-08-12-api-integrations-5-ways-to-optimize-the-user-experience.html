Do your users want to connect your app with the other cloud services they’re using?Are you getting requests from your customers to use data they already have in cloud storage, CRM, marketing automation or other applications?If so, your users are looking for a Cooperative App experience in which your app and the other apps they use all work together – seamlessly.Cooperative App API integrations generally follow a consistent pattern that you can apply in defining the user stories required to develop the cooperative use cases for your application. This series will help you discover your application’s integration pattern by evaluating 10 dimensions in designing integration use cases.
